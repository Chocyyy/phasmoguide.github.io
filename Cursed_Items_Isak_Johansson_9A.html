Cursed Possessions:

Here are all of the spawn locations and information about every cursed item in Phasmophobia.


Music Box:

About the music box:

The music box is the first cursed item. When used while in your hand, the music box will start to play music. While the song is playing, the ghost will also humm along to the music if it within 20m of the playing music box. The music box also has a sanity affect while you are playing the it, draining at a rate of 2.5%/sec, with a total drop of 75% sanity if you listen to the entire song.


Hunts with the music box:

There are many different ways the music box can trigger hunts (and cursed hunts)


Cursed hunts will start when:

  • You throw the music box
  • You move the music box close enough to the ghost - it will start to walk towards the music box, wait a few seconds, then start the cursed hunt

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • The player reaches 0% sanity and music box hasn't finished playing

    No hunt will occur if:

  • The song finishes playing without the ghost ever getting too close to the music box
  • If the song finishes as you just get close enough, the ghost will vanish and the music box will stop playing music and close

    About the haunted mirror:

    The haunted mirror is the second cursed item. When you look into the mirror, the ghost room will be shown. While looking into the mirror, there will be a sanity affect, with whenever you bring it up to start looking into it, a 20% immediate sanity drop and then draining at a rate of 7.5%/sec.


    Hunts with the haunted mirror:

    There are a few ways the haunted mirror can trigger hunts (and cursed hunts)


    Cursed hunts will start when:

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • Normal hunts cannot be started with the ouija board

    No hunt will occur if:

  • If you lift the mirror up to look in it with at least 27% sanity and only have a quick look

    About the ouija board:

    The ouija board is the third cursed item. When you use the ouija board, you can ask the ghost many different questions. Each question has it's own responses and sanity drain.

    • Summoning Circle:

    About the summoning circle:

    The summoning circle is the fourth cursed item. When you light the summoning circle (the five candles around the edges) with an igniter, the ghost will appear in the middle of the circle, do a ghost event for 5 seconds, then start a cursed hunt. For each candle lit, there will be a sanity affect, with lighting 1 candle draining 16% of yur sanity, meaning to light the entire summoning circle, you need a minimum of 80% sanity. If you don't have enough sanity, it won't light.


    Hunts with the summoning circle:

    There is one way the summoning circle can trigger a cursed hunt


    Cursed hunts will start when:

  • You are able to light all the candles and the ghost appears, does the ghost event and then starts the cursed hunt

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • Normal hunts cannot be started with the summoning circle

    No hunt will occur if:

  • If you don't have enough sanity to light all the candles. The game will force all the candles you try and light the get blown out, however, the ones you already lit will sta (There is a way yo bypass this in multiplayer, or even in singleplayer depending on how many candles can be lit)
  • If you do bypass this, you will get a cursed hunt to occur

    • Tarot Cards:

    About the tarot cards:

    The tarot cards are the fifth cursed item. There is a deck of 10 cards that you can pull. There are 10 different cards (with 1 of them having 2 different possible outcomes), and everything (such as sanity drains, hunts all depend on the card you pull.


    Hunts with the tarot cards:

    There is one way tarot cards can trigger a cursed hunt


    Cursed hunts will start when:

  • You pull the "Death" card (more on the types of cards below)

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • Normal hunts cannot be started with the tarot cards

    No hunt will occur if:

  • If you don't pull a "Death" pull


    Different cards you can pull from the tarot cards:

    Here are all of the different possible cards you can pull from the tarot cards (and what they do)!


    The Tower:

  • Force an interaction (It is currently bugged and will rarely actually force and interaction*)
  • Double all ghost activity 20 seconds
  • When you pull it, the card will burn blue
  • 20% chance to draw "The Tower" card
  • Fun Fact: "The Tower" card used to be able to force the evidence "writing" and "DOTS"


    The Wheel of Fortune:

    There are 2 different types of the "Wheel of Fortune" card - a red and green card
    There is a 20% chance to draw any of the "Wheel of Fortune" card

    Red:

    • Drains the person who pulled its' sanity by 25%
    • When you pull it, the card will burn red
    • 10% chance to draw " The Wheel of Fortune - Red" card

    Green:

    • Heals the person who pulled its' sanity by you 25%
    • When you pull it, the card will burn green
    • 10% chance to draw "The Wheel of Fortune - Green" card


    The Hermit:

  • Teleports ghost back to ghost room (Note: Doesn't affect hunts and ghost events)
  • Traps ghost in ghost room for 1 minute (Note: Doesn't affect hunts and ghost events)
  • When you pull it, the card will burn cyan
  • 10% chance to draw "The Hermit" card


    The Sun:

  • Heals the person who pulled its' sanity back up to 100% no matter your current sanity
  • When you pull it, the card will burn yellow
  • 5% chance to draw "The Sun" card


    The Moon:

  • Drains the person who pulled its' sanity all the way down to 0% no matter your current sanity
  • When you pull it, the card will burn white
  • 5% chance to draw "The Moon" card


    The Devil:

  • Forces a ghost event targeting/near the player who pulled the card
  • When you pull it, the card will burn pink
  • 10% chance to draw "The Devil" card


    Death:

  • Forces a cursed hunt
  • When you pull it, the card will burn purple
  • 10% chance to draw the "Death" card


    The High Priestess:

  • Either revives a dead player (if more than 1 it revives the first dead player) OR revives the next player to die
  • When you pull it, the card will burn light yellow
  • 2% chance to draw "The High Priestess" card


    The Hanged Man:

  • Immediately kills the player who pulled the card
  • It does not burn because you immediately die when pulled
  • 1% chance to draw "The Hanged Man" card


    The Fool:

  • Any card has a 17% chance to turn into the fool card - if it does, nothing happens
  • When you pull it, the card will burn light purple
  • 17% chance to draw "The Fool" card - 100% chance during any hunt

    • Voodoo Doll:

    About the voodoo doll:

    The voodoo doll is the sixth cursed item. It has 10 pins all over it's body, with 1 being in it's heart, and when you use it, everytime one of those pins gets push into it. If you push in a normal (not heart) pin ,then it will drain 5% of your sanity. If you push in the heart pin, it will drain 10% of your sanity. If you have less sanity than required for the pin that you push, all pins will immediately push in and it will start a cursed hunt. Fun Fact: The voodoo interaction pins used to be able to force the evidence "writing" and "DOTS".


    Hunts with the voodoo doll:

    There are a few ways the voodoo doll can trigger hunts (and cursed hunts)


    Cursed hunts will start when:

  • You pull the "heart pin" (more on that below)
  • You are too low sanity for the pin that you pull - all pins pushed in and cursed hunt starts

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • Normal hunts cannot be started with the voodoo doll

    No hunt will occur if:

  • If you have enough sanity and pull a "normal" (not heart) pin


    Different pins you can push on the voodoo doll:

    Here are all of the different possible pins you can push on the voodoo doll (and what they do)!


    Body "Normal" Pin:

    • When pushed in, an interaction will occur
    • If you don't have enough sanity to pull it - all pins get pushed in and cursed hunt starts
    • There are 9 body "normal" pins on the voodoo doll
    • Fun Fact: The code used for the tarot card "The Tower" and the voodoo doll body "normal" pins are the exact same


    Heart Pin:

    • When pushed in, a cursed hunt will start
    • If you don't have enough sanity to pull it - all pins get pushed in and cursed hunt starts
    • There is one heart pin on the voodoo doll

    • Monkey Paw:

    About the monkey paw:

    The monkey paw is the seventh and last cursed item., however, it was added later than all the other cursed items. The other cursed items were added in v0.5, whereas monkey paw was added in v0.8.1. You can ask the monkey paw 10 different wishes, however, you don't get to ask all of them. The amount of fingers the monkey paw holds up determines how many wishes you get. How many fingers it holds up is determined by the reward multiplier - x0-x1.99 = 5 wishes, x2-x2.99 = 4 wishes, x3 or higher = 3 wishes. Asking questions works very similarly to the ouija board, however, rather than responding, it has a bigger affect on the game. The sanitydrain also depends on the wish you are asking.


    Hunts with the monkey paw:

    There are many different ways the monkey paw can trigger hunts (and cursed hunts)


    Cursed hunts will start when:

  • You say "I wish to see the ghost"
  • You say "I wish to trap the ghost" OR "I wish the ghost was trapped"

    Normal hunts will start when:

  • You say "I wish for knowledge"

    No hunt will occur if:

  • You wish for any other wish


    Different wishes you can wish for with the monkey paw:

    Here are all of the different possible wishes you make with the monkey paw (and what they do)! (Note: You can only use each wish once - for weather, you can only wish for each weather once)


    Ghost:

      I wish to see the ghost:

      • Triggers a ghost event (visible ghost event where the ghost stands still) at the ghosts current loction
      • The player who made the wish will have obscured vision with dark fog
      • A cursed hunt will then start
      • When the cursed hunt ends, the players vision is returned back to normal


      I wish for activity OR I wish the ghost would do something:

      • Doubles all ghost activity for 2 minutes
      • The breaker will then break and stay broken for the rest of the contract
      • The exit door will be locked during this time


      I wish to trap the ghost OR I wish the ghost was trapped:

      • Teleports the ghost to it's favourite room and locks all the doors around the favourite room for 1 minute
      • All doors around the player will also be locked during this time
      • After this, all doors will unlock and a cursed hunt will start
      • There is a 25% chance for the doors to only unlock after the grace period rather than before it


    Player:

      I wish to be sane OR I wish for sanity:

      • Sets every player's sanity to 50%
      • Passive sanity drain will be multiplied by x1.5 for the rest of the contract
      • Ghosts favourite room will change to a random room (even a Goryo will change it's favourite room)


      I wish to be safe:

      • Opens up the nearest blocked hiding spot
      • The lights in the room of the player who wished it will shatter/break
      • The ghost will be able to detect any equipment or voice of the player who wished it from any distance for the rest of the contract


      I wish to leave:

      • The player who wished this can unlocked the front door during at anytime (even during a hunt)
      • The players speed will be greatly reduced for 5 seconds
      • Their vision will also be obscured with dark fog during this time


    Other:

      I wish for knowledge:

      • Removes (crosses out) any incorrect evidence type and it's corresponding ghost types from the wishers journal
      • The ghost will then start a cursed hunt near the wisher
      • The wisher will get muffled audio/sounds and reduced vision until either death or the end of the contract


      I wish to revive my friend OR I wish for life:

      • Revives a dead player
      • The player who made the wish has a 50/50 chance of dying


      I wish for [weather]:

      • Changes the wether to whatever the player wished for
      • Temporary blindness affect with dark fog while the weather changes
      • Reduces the wishers sanity by 25%

        List of all the possible weathers:

        • Clear Sky
        • Sun
        • Wind
        • Fog
        • Snow
        • Rain

        When you wish for rain, it will be 50/50 chance for if it grants light or heavy rain


      I wish for anything:

      • Randomly grants one of the wishes above
      • Cannot be a used wish

    • Cursed Hunts:

    Cursed hunts are hunts that are caused by a cursed item (note that all cursed items can start dursed hunts, however some of them can also start normal hunts whereas others can't). They also ignore sanity thresholds and hunt cooldowns. Cursed hunts are hunts that can only be stopped by a tier 3 crucifix that has all 2 uses not used. Cursed hunts always last exactly 20 seconds longer than a normal hunt no matter the difficulty and/or map.
