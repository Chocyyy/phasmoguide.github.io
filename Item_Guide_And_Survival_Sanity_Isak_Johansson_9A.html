Flashlight:

The flashlight provides a light source. This is useful for making your way around dark areas when/where lights are unavailable. It is a starter item, meaning you get one for free with every contract.

    • Incense:

    Incense is an item used for getting away in a bad situation during a hunt and to stop the ghost from hunting. The incense does not stop the hunt, it just either repels, slows, or halts the ghost (depending on tier of incense) for a short time for you to get away. It alos temporarily stops the ghost from hunt (this duration is shorter for Demons and longer for Spirits). It is a secondary item, meaning you or your friend/s have to buy and bring an them. Some of the tiers are consumable, meaning when it gets used all up, you have to buy more, even if you don't die.

    • Phasmophobia Sanity + Survival:

    Phasmophobia has a sanity system that is related to survival/hunts.

    Sanity:

    This is how the sanity system works in Phasmophobia!

    For information about survival/hiding, click the link on the image to go to a video guide on every map + all hiding spots (Note: Maple Lodge Campsite has hcanged since this video was made)


    If you are standing in a room with a light on, your sanity will not passivly drain (only ghost events, cursed items, moroi curse, sanity pills, e.t.c can affect your sanity). However, when you are standing in a large rooom (e.g. sunny meadows hallways, high school gym), your sanity will only be prrevented by 20%, with your sanity drain being 80% of what the sanity drain is set to.


    These things can affect your sanity:

  • Sanity pills = +???% (Depends on difficulty/game settings)

  • Ghost Events
    • Oni, any event, any player = -20%
    • Banshee, singing event, only on hunt target player = -15%
    • Anything else = -10%
  • Ghost Abilities
    • Yurei = -15%
    • Moroi = double passive sanity drain + lights no longer prevent sanity drain
    • Jinn = -25%
    • Phantom = ~-0.5%/sec
    • Poltergiest = 2% per item thrown
  • Cursed Item
    • Haunted Mirror: Pulling it up + while looking into it = -20% when pulled up, -7.5%/sec while looking into it
    • Monkey Paw: "I wish to be sane"/"I wish for sanity" = Sanity set to 50% + increases passive drain rate by 50%
    • Music Box: Near active (playing) music box = -2.5%/sec
    • Ouija Board: *Look on "Cursed Item" page for information about ouija boards + sanity drain
    • Summoning Circle: Each candle lit = -16% sanity
    • Tarot Cards: *Look on "Cursed Item" page for information about tarot cards + sanity drain
    • Voodoo Doll: Body "normal" pin = -5%, heart pin = -10%
  • When player dies, all living players = -15% sanity
