About Phasmophobia:

Phasmophobia is a psychological horror game made by Kinetic Games and released on Spetember 18th, 2020 on Steam in early access. This game can be played in both co-op multiplayer (up to 4 players) or in single player, where you and your group of paranormal investigators enter a haunted location with a ghost. The ghost will do paranormal interactions in the location as you try to complete your main objective- identify what type of ghost you are dealing with - by using the equipment you purchased or given to you. Once you identify the type of ghost, you leave the rest of the job up to another paranormal ghost removal team.
