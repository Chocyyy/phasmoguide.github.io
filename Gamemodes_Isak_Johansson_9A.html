Phasmophobia Difficulties:

There are currently 6 different difficulties/gamemodes in Phasmophobia. All gamemodes have different game settings (e.g. breaker is on/off from the start of contract) and different reward multiplier (e.g. x1.0, x2.0, e.t.c). However, 1 of the gamemodes (custom difficulty), you can set your own settings with a custom reward multiplier.

Gamemodes:

Here are all of the game settings and other information about all the gamemodes/difficulties in Phasmophobia.


Amateur:

"Recommended for new ghost hunters!"

Amateur is the easiest and simplest preset difficulty that is meant to be for new players and need a bit more help from the game to complete the contracts.

Here are the game settings for amateur difficulty:

  • Unlocked from when you start playing

  • x1.0 reward multiplier

  • 5 minute setup time (ghost can't hunt)

  • High ghost activity

  • 15 second hunt duration on small maps - 30 seconds on medium map - 40 seconds on big maps

  • 5 second grace period (Cursed Hunt/s = 1 second)

  • No doors will start open

  • Sanity medication will restore 40% sanity

  • Sanity starts at 100%

  • 50% of lost equipment value is returned to the player when killed

  • Breaker starts on

  • Breaker is shown on the truck map

  • x1 sanity drain speed

  • Always has cursed item

  • No hiding spots will be blocked

  • Will show who ghost responds to

  • Ghost room will never change

  • Sanity monitor and activity chart will not be broken

  • Kills will not extend hunts

  • Ghost will show all 3 evidences

  • Fingerprints will disappear after 2 minutes

  • Low ghost event rate




    • Intermediate:

    "The standbrd Ghost Huntin' experience"

    Intermediate is a little bit harder than amateur and is best for the average player who is decently familiar with the games mechanics.

    Here are the game settings for intermediate difficulty:

  • Unlocked at level 10

  • x2.0 reward multiplier

  • 2 minute setup time (ghost can't hunt)

  • Medium ghost activity

  • 20 second hunt duration on small maps - 40 seconds on medium maps - 50 seconds on big maps

  • 4 second grace period (Cursed Hunt/s = 1 second)

  • Few doors will start open

  • Sanity medication will restore 35% sanity

  • Sanity starts at 100%

  • 25% of lost equipment value is returned to the player when killed

  • Breaker starts off

  • Breaker is shown on the truck map

  • x1.5 sanity drain speed

  • Always has cursed item

  • Some hiding spots will be blocked

  • Will show who ghost responds to

  • Ghost room will never change

  • Sanity monitor and activity chart will not be broken

  • Kills will not extend hunts

  • Ghost will show all 3 evidences

  • Fingerprints will disappear after 2 minutes

  • Medium ghost event rate




    • Professional:

    "For experienced Ghost Hunters"

    Professional is third preset difficulty and used to be the hardest/highest preset difficulty you could choose (before v0.4). This difficulty is meant for people who want a challenge without it being too difficult.

    Here are the game settings for professional difficulty:

  • Unlocked at level 20

  • x3.0 reward multiplier

  • No setup timer

  • Low ghost activity

  • 30 second hunt duration on small maps - 50 seconds on medium maps - 60 seconds on big maps

  • 3 second grace period (Cursed Hunt/s = 1 second)

  • More doors will start open

  • Sanity medication will restore 30% sanity

  • Sanity starts at 100%

  • 0% of lost equipment value is returned to the player when killed

  • Breaker starts off

  • Breaker is shown on the truck map

  • x2 sanity drain speed

  • Always has cursed item

  • More hiding spots will be blocked

  • Will not show who ghost responds to

  • Ghost room will rarely change

  • Sanity monitor and activity chart will not be broken

  • Kills will not extend hunts

  • Ghost will show all 3 evidences

  • Fingerprints will disappear after 2 minutes

  • Medium ghost event rate




    • Nightmare:

    "A challenge for the best of the best"

    Nightmare is the second hardest preset difficulty and used to be the hardest/highest preset difficulty you could choose (before v0.8.1). This difficulty is meant for people who want a challenge, and who know each ghosts special abilities and hidden traits (also known as "third evidence").

    Here are the game settings for nightmare difficulty:

  • Unlocked at level 30

  • x4.0 reward multiplier

  • No setup timer

  • Low ghost activity

  • 30 second hunt duration on small maps - 50 seconds on medium maps - 60 seconds on big maps

  • 2 second grace period (Cursed Hunt/s = 1 second)

  • Many doors will start open

  • Sanity medication will restore 25% sanity

  • Sanity starts at 100%

  • 0% of lost equipment value is returned to the player when killed

  • Breaker starts off

  • Breaker is not shown on the truck map

  • x2 sanity drain speed

  • Always has cursed item

  • Many hiding spots will be blocked

  • Will not show who ghost responds to

  • Ghost room will occasionally change

  • Sanity monitor and activity chart will be broken

  • Kills will extend hunts by ~10 seconds

  • Ghost will show only 2 evidences

  • Fingerprints will disappear after 2 minutes

  • High ghost event rate




    • Insanity:

    "Feeling brave? These ghosts don't mess around..."

    Insanity is the hardest preset difficulty and currently is the hardest/highest preset difficulty you could choose (before v0.8.1). This difficulty is meant for people who want a challenge, and who know each ghosts special abilities and hidden traits (also known as "third evidence").

    Here are the game settings for nightmare difficulty:

  • Unlocked at level 40

  • x6.0 reward multiplier

  • No setup timer

  • Low ghost activity

  • 30 second hunt duration on small maps - 50 seconds on medium maps - 60 seconds on big maps

  • 2 second grace period (Cursed Hunt/s = 1 second)

  • Lots of doors will start open

  • Sanity medication will restore 20% sanity

  • Sanity starts at 75%

  • 0% of lost equipment value is returned to the player when killed

  • Breaker starts off

  • Breaker is not shown on the truck map

  • x2 sanity drain speed

  • Never has a cursed item

  • Almost all hiding spots will be blocked

  • Will not show who ghost responds to

  • Ghost room will frequently change

  • Sanity monitor and activity chart will be broken

  • Kills will extend hunts by ~10 seconds

  • Ghost will show only 1 evidenc

  • Fingerprints will disappear after 1 minute

  • High ghost event rate




    • Custom Difficulty:

    "Create your own game!"

    Custom difficulty is a difficulty that allows you to choose your own game settings! With this, you can make your own fun games to play, or make your own challenges! This difficulty is for anybody who wants to create their own difficulty! You can save up to 3 dfferent custom settings and choose to play on any of them! (Note: Custom difficulty is unlocked at level 50).

    Here are some of the settings you can change in the custom difficulty settings:


    Player Settings:

  • Sanity medication restoration amount

  • Starting sanity

  • Sanity drain speed multiplier

  • Spriting use allowance

  • Flashlights being broken

  • Losing items on death

  • Player speed multiplier


    • Ghost Settings:

  • Number of evidence/s given

  • Chance of changing favourite room

  • Interaction amount

  • Event frequency

  • Hunt duration

  • Grace period duration

  • Fingerprint chance

  • Fingerprint duration

  • Disable hunts (Friendly ghosts) - Note: This will result in a x0 reward

  • Kills extending hunts

  • Roaming frequency

  • Ghost speed


    • Contract Settings:

  • Setup time

  • Weather

  • Number of doors starting open

  • Amount of hiding places

  • Sanity monitor state

  • Activity monitor state

  • Fuse box map visibility

  • Fuse box starting state

  • Cursed item/s quantity

  • Cursed item/s choice/s

  • Roaming frequency

  • Ghost speed

    • When you set the settings you want, the multiplier will display at the bottom of the screen. You can have any multiplier between x0-15. However, if you get the ghost wrong, then 50% will be taken away from the multiplier.



    Weekly Challenge:

    "A new challenge every week..."

    The weekly challenge is a preset custom difficulty that is changed every week. There are currently 26 different weekly challenges, and once it cycles through every challenge, it resets from the start, however, more are being added constantly. This challenge gives you a set challenges, set items (including different tiers) and set maps you have to do this challenge on.

    For example, this weeks challenge is "Paranormal Paparazzi". The description is "You've got cameras, lights and sound. Find that ghost type!". You have to go to Bleasdble Farmhouse and are given many different items that either make sound, produce light, and a camera. The game provides the items, so if you die you don't lose anything (and if you get the ghost type correct, it still counts). You have to complete the challenge (get the ghost type right) 3 times a week and you will be rewarded with $5000 and 5000xp. You can do this challenge whenever you want during the week.

    Apocalypse:

    "Try at your own risk..."


    Bronze Trophy:

    To get the bronze trophy, you need to create a custom difficulty with a x6-x10 reward multiplier and:

  • Play on Sunny Meadows
  • Correctly identify the ghost type
  • Complete all 3 objectives
  • Get a ghost photo (of any star)


    • Silver Trophy:

    To get the silver trophy, you need to create a custom difficulty with a x10-x15 reward multiplier and:

  • Play on Sunny Meadows
  • Correctly identify the ghost type
  • Complete all 3 objectives
  • Get a ghost photo (of any star)


    • Gold Trophy:

    To get the gold trophy, you need to create a custom difficulty with a x15 reward multiplier and:

  • Play on Sunny Meadows
  • Correctly identify the ghost type
  • Complete all 3 objectives
  • Get a ghost photo (of any star)
