EMF 5:

EMF level 5 is the first piece of evidence that a ghost can have. It is collected using an item called the "EMF Reader". There are 4 other levels of EMF, each meaning different things:

EMF Level 1: The device is on

EMF Level 2: Ghost Interaction and/or Ability

EMF Level 3: Ghost threw an object

EMF Level 4: Ghost Manifested (Ghost Event)

EMF Level 5: Evidence

EMF level 5 has a 25% chance of happening instead of EMF 2 and/or 3
Only tier 2 EMF reader shows the corrosponding colour above to the tiers

  • Tier 1 EMF shows the actual numbers and some quiet sounds, with EMF 5 being louder than the other 4 levels
  • Tier 2 EMF shows the colour for each of the different levels, with blue being the lowest and red being the highest. Tier 2 also makes a "ringing" sound
  • Tier 3 EMF shows a bar at the top. The more coloured in (with black) the bar is, the higher the level. Tier 3 makes a beeping sound, and mostly, the faster the beeps, the higher the EMF level
  • Tier 3 EMF can can also show up to 3 different readings, whereas the previous tiesr could only show 1
  • Tier 3 EMF also shows the distance to the EMF reading from where the player holding it is standing
  • Tier 3 EMF also shows the direction the EMF reading is in in relation to the player holding it
    EMF 5 also cannot occur during a hunt, while all other levels can

    Another helpful way but less reliable way to obtain EMF 5 is through the activity monitor in the truck!
    This activity monitor displays the amount of activity that the ghost is causing
    If you see a random (not occuring from a ghost event/manifestation) spike in acitivty from 4-5 (not the activity just slowly increasing, but a jump by 4-5 in activity), this is most likely EMF 5
    However, it is not always 100% true, so don't just use the activity chart to rely on EMF 5!
    This acitivty chart can also be used to detect the Poltergeist's ability ("multi-throw"/"Polter throw"/"Polty bomb") and the Twins ability ("twinteraction")
  • The Poltergeist "multi-throw" can be seen when the activty randomly spikes up to a 10 without a hunt and in one "reading"/"update"
  • The Twins "twinteraction" can be seen when the activity does not have a continuous gradient (it plateaus but immediately continues increasing)
  • A hunt can also be detected on the activity chart, as hunts will always make the activity go up to 10 (note: all hunts will do this, but not every 10 activity is a hunt)

    Spirit Box:

    Spirit box is the second piece of evidence that a ghost can have. It is collected using an item called the "Spirit Box". There are many different questions and response possible to ask/recive, including:

    Agressive" Questions:

    "Are you friendly?"
    "What do you want?"
    "Why are you here?"
    "Do you want to hurt us?"
    "Should we leave?"

    Location Questions:

    "Where are you?"
    "Are you close?"
    "Are you here?"
    "Is there a ghost here?"
    "What is your location?"

    Age Questions:

    "How old are you?"
    "Are you a child?"
    "Are you old?"
    "Are you young?"

    Miscellaneous:

    "Give us a sign"
    "Speak to us"
    "Can you talk?"
    "Show yourself"
    "How many pickles can you fit up your butt?"


    There are many factors that depend on if you get a response, such as:
  • The tier of spirit box you have (tier 1 (low), 2 (medium) or 3 (high) - the exact chance of getting a response is unknown, but the higher the tier, the higher the chance of getting a response)
  • The range of the spirit box/How close you are to the ghost (tier 1 - 3m, tier 2 - 4m, tier 3 - 5m) (Note: You must also be in the same room/area as the ghost)
  • The main lights in the room you are asking the question in must be off (not including equipment, small lamps and/or other rooms lights)
  • Responding to people who are alone or everyone
    • If the ghost prefers people alone, only 1 person can be in the room alone to get a response
    • If the ghost prefers a group, any amount of players can be in the room at once
    • If the difficulty has a setup timer (e.g. Amateur, Intermediate, and custom difficulty with setup timer on and >0 seconds) the board in the truck will say either of these
    • However, if the setup time if off (e.g. Professional, Nightmare, Insanity, custom difficulty with steup time off), the board will not say this information
    • However, you can find this out by either testing with the spirit box yourself, or asking the Ouija Board (if you have this cursed item) and ask it "Do you respond to everyone?"
    • If no, it prefers people who are alone, if yes, it prefers any amount of people

    • If you don't know if the spirit box heard you in game, you can:
  • Ask it any valid question (e.g. "Where are you?")
  • On tier 1:
      . The white light next the "microphone" will appear when the spirit box in on (and push to talk/toggle is on/activated)
      . If it heard you but you got no response, the dot next to the "ghost" symbol will light up red
      . If you got a response, the dot next to the "ghost" symbol will light up and start flashing white as well as you hearing a voice
  • On tier 2:
      . The "microphone" symbol at the top left will turn black when the spirit box is on (and push to talk/toggle is on/activated)
      . If it heard you but you got no response, the "X" symbol will turn black
      . If you got a response, the "ghost" symbol will turn black and a voice can be heard
  • On tier 3:
      . The "power" button will turn red when the spirit box is on
      . The "mic input" button will turn white while mic is on (push to talk/toggle is on/activated)
      . If it heard you but you got no response, the "response" bar will turn red
      . If you got a response, the "response" bar will turn white and a voice will be heard

    • Special Abilities:

    2 ghosts in Phasmophobia have special abilities that relate to the use of the spirit box:

    The Moroi and Deogen

  • Moroi:
    • The Moroi will double your sanity drain when you get a response on the spirit box
    • The curse will last for the entire round until you take a sanity medication
    • The curse will drain your sanity even if you are standing in a room with the main light on (which would normal not drain your sanity at all) or holding a candle (which prevents some sanity drain)
    • The curse can affect multiple people at once, however, cannot stack onto 1 player

  • Deogen:
    • The Deogen will provide a special "heavy breathing" response when standing within 1m of the ghost and you get a response (note: this has a 33% chance of occuring instead of any normal response)
    • This does not affect anything in the game

    Ghost Writing:

    Ghost writing is the third piece of evidence that a ghost can have. It is collected using an item called the "Ghost Writing Book". There are many different things a ghost can write.

    What the ghost chooses to write in the book is random and doesn't mean anything
    The chance for a ghost to write in book depends on the tier of writing book

    Tier 1 - 33% chance for the ghost to write when interacting with the book

    Tier 2 - 50% chance for the ghost to write when interacting with the book

    Tier 3 - 66% chance for the ghost to write when interacting with the book

    The ghost must have ghost writing on top of the chance to write in it

    The ghost writing book also must be placed in the room for the ghost to be able to write in it
    The ghost cannot write in the book during a hunt, and will instead throw the writing book

    Helpful tip:

    If the ghost throws the book and has not yet written in it, the ghost will not have ghost writing as an evidence
    This is because the ghost will always prioritise writing in it over throwing it
    Only works on Amateur, Intermediate, Professional, or a custom difficulty with 3 evidences. This is because if the ghost has "ghost writing" as an evidence, and chooses to hide that evidence in the game, then the ghost will be unable to write in it and will always throw the book

    You can also get 2 photos off of any ghost writing book that has been written in:
  • You can get an interaction photo that must be taken within a few seconds of the ghost writing in the book
  • And you can also get a ghost writing photo that can be taken once per book (that has been written in) and can be taken whenever

    Freezing Temperatures:

    Freezing temperatures is the fourth piece of evidence that a ghost can have. It is collected using an item called a "Thermometer". Different ghosts make rooms different temperatures.

    For a room to be considered "freezing", the room temperature needs to be below 0¬∞C

    The specific temperature does not matter as to what ghost type it is (however, Hantu speed will change depending on the temperature of the room they are in during a hunt
    Note: More on Hantu and how the temperature changes their speed on the "Ghost Types" page

    The ghost room temperature will be anywhere below 5¬∞C for a non-freezing ghost, and blow 0¬∞C for a freezing ghost
    Hantu does not make a room colder than any other ghost type

    Freezing/Cold Breath:

    • In any room that is below 5¬∞C, you will see your own breath called "cold breath". This does not mean freezing (However, before v0.9.1, this would mean it is freezing)
    • This also does not always mean you are in the ghost room, as if the weather outside is snowing (and the breaker is off), it will make the rooms colder, and show freezing breath everywhere
    • This also includes in the truck/outside the haunted location
    • However, with most other weathers, the ghost room will almost always be the only room with cold breath (if the breaker in on)

    Ghost Orbs:

    These orbs can only be seen on the video camera or on the computer screen/video footage in the truck
    This is only if the map comes with video surveillance footage cameras set up or if the players place a video camera either down on the ground or set it up of a tripod

    Ultraviolet:

    Ultraviolet is the sixth piece of evidence that a ghost can have. It is collected using an item called a "Ultraviolet (UV) Light". Ultraviolet can be collected through fingerprints and/or foootsteps.

    Looking for UV:

    UV can be found through multiple means. Here are the 2 different ways to find it:

  • Fingerprints

    • Fingerprints can be found on:

      • Most types of doors
      • Light Switches (Except most lamps without seperate switches)
      • Windows (Including glass doors)
      • Computer Keyboards
      • Cooler Boxes
      • Mirrors (Specific maps only)
      • Horizontal surface of a bar cell door (Prison)
      • Steel Keyhole surfaces on cell doors (Prison)
      • Bases of lanterns
      • Interior edges around large tent door flaps/tops of small tent door flaps
      • Picnic Benchtops
      • String Light Poles

    • (Any touches of lights/doors that occur during ghost events will not leave fingerprints on the objects)

    • Fingerprints will disspear in 2 minutes on most difficulties, but 1 minute on insanity (unless using custom difficulty)

    • Obake

      • The Obake has fingerprints as an evidence, however, it is a special ghost

      • The Obake has an approximately 16.7% chance to leave a "non-standard" fingerprint. The "non-standard" fingerprints include:

        • A fingerprint with 6 fingers instead of 5 on doors, windows/glass doors, cooler boxes, bases of lanterns, interior edges around large tent door flaps/ tops of small tent door flaps, picnic benchtops and string light poles
        • Two fingers on light switches (except lamps without seperate switches)
        • 5 fingers on keyboards and Prison doors instead of 4

      • The Obake will make fingerprints dissapear faster on any surface (exact time is unkown)
      • The Obake also has an unkown chance to not leave a fingerprint while interacting with objects, even on 1 or more evidence

  • Footsteps

    • Salt

      • When a ghost steps in salt, if the ghost doesn't have UV evidence, it won't leave footsteps
      • If the ghost does ave UV evidence, it will leave a footstep wherever it steps
      • Obake will leave fewer footsteps than other ghosts

    • All forms of UV have to be "charged" by a UV light for their picture to be taken
    • To charge any form of UV:

      • Tier 1:

        • Shake the glowstick up and drop it next to the object so you can still see the UV
        • Take a picture of the salt pile that was stepped in for a "disturbed salt" photo (if you got footsteps as UV - doesn't need UV charging) as well as the footstep photo
        • If you got fingerprints, take a fingerprint photo (and an interaction photo if your not too late

      • Tier 2:

        • Shine the UV light at the door for ~5 seconds
        • Drop it, and quickly take a picture for the footstep/fingerprint photo and the interaction/disturbed salt picture

      • Tier 3:

        • Same as tier 2, except the tier 3 doesn't take as long to charge, and it is charged for longer

  • Photos from UV:

    • Fingerprints (UV)
    • Footsteps (UV)
    • Interaction (From anything the ghost touches/moves - but take the photo quick)
    • Disturbed Salt (stepped in salt piles)

    • DOTS:

    The ghost can enter the DOTS state whereever, however, it isn't very common, and it is recommended to put the DOTS in the ghost room anyways, as that is where the ghost is most of the time and will have the highest chance to catch the DOTS.

    There is one ghost that has different interactions to the other ghosts with DOTS evidence, the Goryo

    The Goryo DOTS can only be seen through any video camera while no player is in the ghost room

    This makes almost every player hate the Goryo due to how annoying it is to get DOTS (especially on bigger maps)

    When the ghost enters DOTS state (it only enters it for a few seconds), it will roam towards the closest player, or, if it's a Banshee, it will roam towards a target.

    Fake Orbs:

    "Fake" Orbs is another "unofficial" piece of evidence that only a Mimic can have. It is the exact same as an Orb (more about Orbs above). However, the evidence is also a bit different to normal orbs.

    The Mimic will always show orbs + any other evidence the Mimic has (Spirit Box, Freezing Temperatures, Ultraviolet) - Here is the evidence The Mimic will show:

  • 0 Evidence: "Fake" Orbs only
  • 1 Evidence: "Fake" Orbs + 1 other evidence (will choose 1 random evidence out of spirit box, freezing temperatures or ultraviolet
  • 2 Evidence: "Fake" Orbs + 2 other evidences (will choose 2 random evidences out of spirit box, freezing temperatures or ultraviolet)
  • 3 Evidence: "Fake" Orbs + 3 other evidences (will choose all 3 evidence, being spirit box, freezing temperatures or ultraviolet



    • Forced Evidence:

    Forced evidence is 1 evidence that certain ghost will force to make one of their evidences (if there is evidences avaliable to collect).

    For example:

  • If on 0 evidence, the only ghost with evidence will be Mimic with "fake" orbs (more on "fake" orbs above)
  • If on 1 evidence, Hantu will always have freezing
  • if on 2 evidence, Hantu will always have freezing and one other random evidence (either ultraviolet or ghost orbs)
  • If on 3 evidence, Hantu will have all evidences (freezing temperatures, ultraviolet and ghost orbs)

    • This will occur for all ghosts with forced evidence. These ghosts being:

  • Moroi (Spirit Box)
  • Deogen (Spirit Box)
  • Hantu (Freezing Temperatures)
  • Obake (Ultraviolet - however it does have a chance to not show fingerprints or show less footsteps)
  • Goryo (DOTS)
  • Mimic ("Fake" Orbs - more on that above)
