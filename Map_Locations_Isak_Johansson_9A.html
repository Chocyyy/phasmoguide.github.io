Phasmophobia Maps:

There are currently 12 different unique maps in Phasmophobia. All maps also vary in size, with 8 small maps, 2 medium maps and 2 large maps. The size of the map affects the money and experience gain, sanity drain and light limit (how many lights you can have on at once before the breaker breaks).

Maps*:

Here are all of the layouts, breaker/cursed item spawns, photos, and other information about all the maps in Phasmophobia.


Small Maps:

Here are all of the layouts, breaker/cursed item spawns, photos/screenshots, and other information about the small maps in Phasmophobia.

Extra Info:

Payout (Money & Experience):

  • Ghost Type Correct: $100 / 100xp
  • Bone Collected: $25 / 25xp

    Lights:
  • 9 lights can be on at once before the breaker will break

    Sanity Drain:
  • While the setup timer is still going, the sanity drain rate will be -0.09%/second, and when the timer has run out or if there was no timer, it will be changed to -0.12%/s when not standing in a room with the main light on
    Note that money pay and sanity drain will change based on difficulty/multiplier, which will be explained on the "Gamemodes" page.


    6 Tanglewood Drive:

    About Tanglewood:

    Tanglewood was the first map added to Phasmophobia, however, has had a layout change in patch "0.6.1", where the utility room was changed and the door moved to face the basement door. It is the smallest house map, and is unlocked from the start of the game. It contains 1 floor + 1 basement (13 rooms total with 11 possible ghost rooms) and has 2 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and 1 key for the car to turn it off if the ghost triggers the alarm. It has 1 possible exit door you can leave the house from.

    13 Willow Street:

    About Willow:

    Willow was not originally in the game. It was added with the patch "0.29", and had a slight change with the garage doors in a later update. It is the second smallest house map and is unlocked at level 9. It contains 1 floor + 3 rooms in the basement (10 rooms total with 10 possible ghost rooms) and has 2 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and 1 key for the car to turn it off if the ghost triggers the alarm. It has 1 possible exit door you can leave the house from.

    10 Ridgeview Court:

    About Ridgeview:

    Ridgeview was in the game since the games release, however, has had a layout change on the first floor in patch "0.6.1", where the kitchen, dining room and living room were switched around to make the map feel more practical. It is the largest house map and is unlocked at level 2. It contains 2 floors + 1 basement (16 rooms total) and has 2 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and 1 key for the car to turn it off if the ghost triggers the alarm (it is the only map with 2 cars, however only 1 can be interacted with by the player and ghost). It has 1 possible exit door you can leave the house from.

    42 Edgefield Road:

    About Edgefield:

    Edgefield was in the game since the games release and has had no layout changes*. It is the same size as Ridgeview, however, feels smaller due to how closed in the map feels. It is unlocked at level 2. It contains 2 floors + 1 basement (16 rooms total) and has 2 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and 1 key for the car to turn it off if the ghost triggers the alarm. It has 1 possible exit door you can leave the house from (and 1 exit door you cannot interact with/open).

    Grafton Farmhouse:

    About Grafton:

    Grafton was in the game since the games release and has had no layout changes*. It is 1 of the 2 farmhouse maps and is the smallest. It is unlocked at level 4. It contains 2 floors and no basements (13 rooms total) and has 3 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 2 possible exit doors you can leave the house from.

    Bleasdale Farmhouse:

    About Bleasdale:

    Bleasdale was in the game since the games release and has had no layout changes*. It is the second of the 2 farmhouse maps and is the biggest. It is unlocked at level 4. It contains 2 floors, no basements and 1 attic (it is the only map with an attic) (16 rooms total) and has 3 different possible breaker spawns, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 3 possible exit doors you can leave the house from.

    Camp Woodwind:

    About Woodwind:

    Camp Woodwind was not originally in the game. It was added with the patch "0.7". It is the first of the 2 campsite/outdoor maps and is the smallest map in the entire game. It is unlocked at level 13. It contains 1 campfire and 1 toilet block (11 rooms total) and has 1 breaker spawn, 1 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 1 possible exit gate you can leave the campsite from.

    Layout of Woodwind:


    Screenshots:






    Sunny Meadows (Asylum) Restricted:

    About Sunny Meadows (Asylum) Restricted:

    Sunny Meadows was not originally in the game. It was added with the patch "0.7" and has 2 different versions - the full Sunny Meadows and a restricted version for people who don't like big maps. It is unlocked at level 17. It contains 1 of 5 different hallways within the map with the other 4 being blocked (the unlocked hallway is random. It has a different amount of rooms depending on the hallway that is unlocked. It has 5 different breaker spawns (depending on the hallway that is unlocked), 3-4 video surveillance cameras footage already installed and no keys. It has 1 possible door you can leave the asylum from.

    • Medium Maps:

    Here are all of the layouts, breaker/cursed item spawns, photos/screenshots, and other information about the medium maps in Phasmophobia.

    Extra Info:

    Payout (Money & Experience):

  • Ghost Type Correct: $125 / 125xp
  • Bone Collected: $35 / 35xp

    Lights:
  • 8 lights can be on at once before the breaker will break

    Sanity Drain:
  • While the setup timer is still going, the sanity drain rate will be -0.05%/second, and when the timer has run out or if there was no timer, it will be changed to -0.08%/s when not standing in a room with the main light on
    Note that money pay and sanity drain will change based on difficulty/multiplier, which will be explained on the "Gamemodes" page.


    Maple Lodge Campsite:

    About Maple Lodge:

    Maple Lodge Campsite was not originally in the game. It was added with the patch "0.4" and had a whole map layout change in patch "0.9.1". It is the second of the 2 campsite/outdoor maps and is the biggest campsite map. It is unlocked at level 19. It contains 2 campfires, 1 toilet block, 1 dock looking out to the water, 1 cabin and 1 reception(28 rooms total) and has 5 different possible breaker spawns, 2 video surveillance camera footage already installed and 1 key to unlock the door to the cabin. It has 1 possible exit gate you can leave the campsite from.

    Prison:

    About Prison:

    Prison was not originally in the game. It was added with the patch "0.2". It is unlocked at level 15. It contains 2 floors (with some areas overlooking the prison blocks and cafeteria, 2 prison blocks (A and B) and 1 cafeteria (31 rooms total - 29 possible ghost rooms) and has 5 different possible breaker spawns, 11 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 1 possible exit gate you can leave the prison from.

    • Large Maps:

    Here are all of the layouts, breaker/cursed item spawns, photos/screenshots, and other information about the large maps in Phasmophobia.

    Extra Info:

    Payout (Money & Experience):

  • Ghost Type Correct: $150 / 150xp
  • Bone Collected: $50 / 50xp

    Lights:
  • 7 lights can be on at once before the breaker will break

    Sanity Drain:
  • While the setup timer is still going, the sanity drain rate will be -0.03%/second, and when the timer has run out or if there was no timer, it will be changed to -0.05%/s when not standing in a room with the main light on
    Note that money pay and sanity drain will change based on difficulty/multiplier, which will be explained on the "Gamemodes" page.


    Brownstone High School:

    About High School:

    High School was in the game since the games release and has had no layout changes*. However, in patch "0.9", High School was changed to be classified as a "large" map (it used to be "medium"). It is unlocked at level 11. It contains 2 floors and 1 gym (64 rooms total - 58 possible ghost rooms) and has 3 different possible breaker spawns, 15 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 5 possible exit doors you can leave the school from.

    About Sunny Meadows:

It is unlocked at level 17. It contains 2 floors, 1 courtyard, 1 mourge and 1 chapel (69 rooms total) and has 5 different possible breaker spawns, 8 video surveillance camera footage already installed and no keys. It has 1 possible exit door you can leave the asylum from.
